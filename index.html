About Me

I am currently a Principal Engineer at Dropbox where I work on Persistent Systems.

Previously, for 13 years, I worked at Google on Google Cloud Storage.

I received my PhD from Princeton University in Computer Science where I was advised by Michael J. Freedman. I graduated from University of Massachusetts Amherst in 2007 with a B.S. in Computer Science and Mathematics.

I have a personal blog called Programming Sanity.
